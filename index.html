Frequently Asked Questions

How many messages can I send per day?

You can send up to 15 messages per day. This limit helps ensure high-quality conversations and optimal performance for all users.

When do my messages reset?

Your daily message limit resets at midnight (12:00 AM) in your local time zone. This means you'll get a fresh set of 15 messages each day.

Is my data private?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously. We don't store any conversation data permanently, and all communications are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

How can I make the most of my daily messages?

To get the most value from your conversations:

Be specific in your questions and prompts
Provide context when needed
Use follow-up questions to dive deeper into topics
Check your remaining messages in the profile section
